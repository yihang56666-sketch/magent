# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be practical, respectful, and welcoming for people who
are learning agent workflows as well as people hardening them for production.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear, kind, and specific.
- Critique ideas and patches, not people.
- Keep examples safe to share; avoid secrets and private task data.
- Respect maintainers' time by including reproduction steps and verification
  output when reporting problems.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Publishing private credentials, prompts, or generated run artifacts
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith comments

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation for behavior that harms the project.
